Who We Are

Platform Capital is a markets focused, sector agnostic, principal investment and advisory firm.

Platform deploys patient, value accretive capital alongside international and local value investors to create champion businesses with the potential for regional or global scale.

Our Value Proposition

Experienced Team with Strong Track Record and Alignment

Highly focused, top calibre investment professionals; who have skin-in-the-game and are aligned with investors and companies.

Access to Global High Quality Deal Flow

Private opportunities sourced through our global network that do not fit the mandates of many traditional investment firms.

Unique Investment Strategy with Value Addition at its Core

The core of what we do is to create fast-growing, market-leading companies in their various segments. We leverage our local presence, global network, operating experience, technical partnerships and market knowledge to add value to our portfolio and clients’ businesses.

Attractive Investment Structures

Exposure through investment structures capable of providing downside protection whilst unlocking equity upside across all stages of the business cycle.

Our Strategy

We Own & Operate • We Invest • We Advise

We Own & Operate

We operate assets in underserved verticals with high demand-supply gaps. We use our network’s experience to derisk, build infrastructure, and retain value on the continent.

We Invest

We invest in upstream, downstream, and service sectors through direct investments and third-party vehicles that align with our vision for Africa’s future.

We Advise

We provide end-to-end advisory on fundraising, governance, talent, and strategy — empowering entrepreneurs across Africa to scale and succeed.
