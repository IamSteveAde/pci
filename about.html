About Us

Our Mission

To build the leading growth market focused, Investment & Advisory firm, generating globally competitive returns by combining a deep knowledge of the markets we operate in, an entrepreneurial business-building approach and strong local and global connectivity

Who We Are

Platform Capital is a growth markets focused, sector agnostic, principal investment and advisory firm.

Platform deploys patient, value accretive capital alongside international and local value investors to create champion businesses with the potential for regional or global scale.

Platform also provides end-to-end bespoke Corporate Finance & Capital Markets advisory services.

What makes us different is our obsession with changing the African narrative.

Our Value Proposition

Experienced Team with Strong Track Record and Alignment

Highly focused, top calibre investment professionals; who have skin-in-the-game and are aligned with investors and companies.

Access to Global High Quality Deal Flow

Private opportunities sourced through our global network that do not fit the mandates of many traditional investment firms.

Unique Investment Strategy with Value Addition at its Core

The core of what we do is to create fast-growing, market-leading companies in their various segments. We leverage our local presence, global network, operating experience, technical partnerships and market knowledge to add value to our portfolio and clients’ businesses.

Attractive Investment Structures

Exposure through investment structures capable of providing downside protection whilst unlocking equity upside across all stages of the business cycle.

Our Strategy

We Own & Operate • We Invest • We Advise

We Own & Operate

We operate assets in underserved verticals with high demand-supply gaps. We use our network’s experience to derisk, build infrastructure, and retain value on the continent.

We Invest

We invest in upstream, downstream, and service sectors through direct investments and third-party vehicles that align with our vision for Africa’s future.

We Advise

We provide end-to-end advisory on fundraising, governance, talent, and strategy — empowering entrepreneurs across Africa to scale and succeed.

What We Believe

We believe the Africa of tomorrow will emerge as the right ideas meet ideal capital in an ecosystem that engenders growth and scale

Platform Capital: What We Do

01

OWN & OPERATE

We own and operate assets in verticals with massive gaps between demand and supply. By leveraging our team's skills, knowledge, and experience, we build companies that create lasting value and scale across the continent.

02

INVEST

We invest in existing businesses across upstream, downstream, and service segments of critical verticals. Our investments aim to build and scale companies that are integral to Africa's growth and future development.

03

ADVICE

We provide entrepreneurs with expert advice in areas such as fundraising, strategy, scaling, talent management, and governance, empowering them to drive meaningful change and success across Africa.

Investments: How We Invest

01. Growth Thesis Driven

We invest in growth opportunities across several sectors and markets.

02. Hands-on Approach

We act as Investpreneurs, investing our own capital, and providing strategic and execution support.

03. Investment in All Stages

We invest at all stages of the business lifecycle, excluding turnaround and arbitrage situations.

04. Long-Term Focus

Our investment approach encourages long-term thinking in both business operations and capital deployment.

05. Value Centric

We adopt a value-accretive approach that prioritizes sustainable business growth and profitability.
