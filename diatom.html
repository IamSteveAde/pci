Diatom Impact

Strategic Impact for a Transformed Africa

A Think Tank for Change, A Catalyst for Progress

Diatom Impact is an initiative of Platform Capital, designed to deliver intentional, data-driven, and inclusive interventions that shape Africa’s future.

About Diatom Impact

Engineered for Impact, Driven by Intellect

Diatom Impact functions as a deliberate mechanism within Platform Capital’s ecosystem — built to translate strategy into measurable outcomes across Africa’s most pressing developmental gaps.

Structured to address systemic challenges, Diatom Impact deploys research, community engagement, and institutional partnerships to deliver bold, scalable solutions that redefine what meaningful impact looks like.

Areas of Focus

Education for Innovation

Programs curated to spark digital literacy, foster entrepreneurial thinking, and empower the next generation with STEM and vocational skills that align with Africa’s evolving job market.

Justice & Gender Equity

Initiatives shaped to strengthen access to justice, reduce gender-based violence, and train public institutions on inclusive practices — all built on data, dignity, and dialogue.

Research That Moves Systems

Actionable, localized research designed to influence policy, guide philanthropy, and support evidence-based development strategies.

Corporate & Institutional Impact

Diatom Impact enables corporations and institutions to implement strategic CSR, ESG, and public engagement efforts with long-term value — moving from performative to transformative.

Our Purpose

Africa’s challenges are complex and interconnected. Diatom Impact exists to tackle these issues systemically—bridging the gap between vision and implementation.

How We Operate

01. Research & Insight

We uncover data-driven opportunities through deep contextual research that informs effective action.

02.Community Engagement

We work alongside communities to ensure solutions are locally relevant and culturally aligned.

03. Institutional Partnerships

We collaborate with government bodies, private sector leaders, and development organizations to co-create programs with reach and impact.

What Makes Us Different

Execution with Integrity

We move beyond ideas to real-world implementation

Systems-Level Thinking

We go beneath symptoms to address root causes.

Scalability at the Core

Every initiative is built with the potential to grow, replicate, and evolve.

The Diatom Vision

To redefine what meaningful, measurable, and sustainable impact looks like in Africa—by building systems that last and empowering communities to thrive.
